You must have plenty of sea-room to tell the truthTruth in; especially, when it seems to have an aspect of newness, as America did in 1492, tho' it was then just as old, and perhaps older than Asia, only the those sagacious philosophers, the common sailors, had never seen it before ; swearing it was all water & moonshine there. （to be concluded next week)
